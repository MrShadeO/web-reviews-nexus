Welcome to the Web Reviews Nexus! Have you ever heard of a famous media title, such as The Lord of the Rings, and wondered if you'd rather read the books, watch the movies, or even the TV show (that's right, one really exists)? Then you will find the Nexus to be a useful tool! Search media titles (including books, movies, and tv shows) and compare review scores for each media type associated with the title.
