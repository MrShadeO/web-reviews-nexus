Web Reviews Nexus

Welcome to the Web Reviews Nexus! Have you ever heard of a famous media title, such as The Lord of the Rings, and wondered if you'd rather read the books or watch the movies? Then you will find the Nexus to be a useful tool! Search media titles (including books, movies, and tv shows) and compare reviews for each media type associated with the title.

About the Nexus

This app is powered by goodreads for book searches and The Movie DB for movie and tv searches. Links to their full websites can be found below.

goodreads: https://www.goodreads.com/

The Movie DB: https://www.themoviedb.org/
